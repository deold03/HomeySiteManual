Common questions

The questions I get most.

The advertised number isn't the real number. Large property management companies quote a low base rate, then bill separately for site visits ($50-100 each), consumables restocking with markup, vendor coordination fees, and a long list of extras that add up fast. My 20% is everything: site visits, restocking, guest communications, calendar and pricing management, and trusted vendor referrals when you need them. There are no surprise line items on your monthly statement because there are no extras to add.

Included: 24/7 guest support, site visits and guest-issue triage, consumables restocking, calendar and pricing strategy, listing optimization across all platforms, and trusted vendor referrals when something needs fixing.

Not included: I don't carry the cost of maintenance or repairs. When something needs work, I'll refer you to vendors I've worked with and trust, and coordinate the introduction. Scheduling and payment happen directly between you and the vendor. That's intentional. You stay in control of the spending, and there's no markup from me on top.

It depends on where you're starting. If the property is already furnished and you just need licensing and a listing, photography and listing setup can be done in a few days. The Chicago STR license takes about a week to process. If you're starting from an empty unit, furnishing and styling is the biggest variable. That can take a few weeks to a couple of months depending on scope.

No long-term lock-in. You can cancel at any time. The contract exists to set clear expectations about what's my responsibility and what's yours, not to trap you in a relationship. If it's not working, we have an honest conversation and unwind cleanly.
