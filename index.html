Meet your host

Hi, I'm Danny.

I've been hosting on Airbnb since 2013 — first in a Brooklyn apartment, now from my 1906 historic graystone in Logan Square. Eleven years in, my flagship listing sits at 4.99 stars with over 160 reviews and ranks in the top 1% of Airbnb homes nationwide. That experience didn't come from a template. It came from genuinely loving the work.

Before this, I spent four years at Goldman Sachs in real estate investing and eight years in corporate strategy. I'm also a licensed Chicago realtor. When I take on a property, I'm not just managing a listing — I'm operating an asset I understand from every angle.

Homey Homes & Co. is small on purpose. I personally oversee every property I host. That's the whole point.
